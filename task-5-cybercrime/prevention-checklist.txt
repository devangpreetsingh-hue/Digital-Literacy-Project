Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Always verify links before clicking
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Regularly check bank statements
- Install apps only from trusted sources
